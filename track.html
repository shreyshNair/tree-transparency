Track Your Plant's Growth

Plant Overview

Welcome to your plant growth tracker! Below, you will see the different stages of growth that your plant has gone through. Each stage comes with a visual representation of your plant's progress over time. Keep track of your plant's journey with each milestone!

Stage 1: Seedling

This is when your plant just began to sprout! It's fragile but full of potential. The seedling stage is critical for root development and early growth.

Stage 2: Early Growth

Your plant is now growing stronger and taller. The first set of leaves appear, and the plant starts to show signs of healthy growth.

Stage 3: Mature Plant

The plant is now fully developed, with a sturdy stem and fully formed leaves. It's thriving and ready for new growth or flowers, depending on the species.

Stage 4: Flowering (If applicable)

Your plant has reached the blooming stage. The flowers start to open, and it's a clear sign of healthy development and maturity.

Stage 5: Fully Grown & Harvest (If applicable)

Your plant is now fully grown. If it’s a fruit or vegetable plant, it may be time to harvest! If it’s ornamental, it's at its peak beauty.
